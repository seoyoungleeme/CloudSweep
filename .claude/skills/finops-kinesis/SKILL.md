# FinOps Kinesis Analysis Skill

## Scope

> **Review-only authority:** LangGraph owns detection and calculations. Review latency, lag, and throughput safety; do not rerun rule arithmetic.

Reduce avoidable stream, shard, retention, and consumer cost while preserving
producer throughput, consumer latency, replay requirements, and operational safety.

## Input Mode

- **Orchestrator subagent mode** (`=== TERRAFORM ===` present): inline slices only.
- **Standalone mode**: scan `WORK_DIR` for full inputs.

## Detection Rules (apply `rules/efo_waste.json`)

| Rule | Condition | Severity | Action |
|------|-----------|----------|--------|
| K1 | EFO enabled but no low-latency, high fan-out, or read-throughput contention evidence | MEDIUM | REVIEW_EFO |
| K2 | Provisioned shards exceed p95 write/read throughput, no throttling, safe headroom | HIGH | REDUCE_SHARDS |
| K3 | Billing mode appears inefficient for observed traffic shape | LOW | MODEL_BILLING_MODE |
| K4 | Extended retention enabled without replay/compliance evidence | MEDIUM | REVIEW_RETENTION |
| K5 | Write/read throttles, high iterator age, or consumer lag present | INFO | DO_NOT_DOWNSIZE_REVIEW_PERFORMANCE |

## Safety Guardrails

- Do not disable EFO or reduce shards based on processing interval alone. EFO
  can be justified by low-latency SLA, consumer isolation, high fan-out,
  read-throughput contention, or critical consumers.
- Shard changes can throttle producers/consumers if peak throughput is not modeled.

Full pre-change checklists: `references/details.md` § Required Safety Checks.

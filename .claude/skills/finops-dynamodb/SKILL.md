# FinOps DynamoDB Analysis Skill

## Scope

Reduce unused provisioned capacity and avoid expensive billing-mode choices
without increasing throttling risk or breaking workload reliability.

## Input Mode

- **Orchestrator subagent mode** (`=== TERRAFORM ===` present): inline slices only.
- **Standalone mode**: scan `WORK_DIR` for full inputs.

## Required Evidence

| File | Used For | If Missing |
|------|----------|------------|
| `main.tf` | `aws_dynamodb_table`, GSI, autoscaling resources | Cannot analyze; ask for path |
| `metrics.json` | Consumed RCU/WCU, throttled requests, successful requests, account/table capacity | Mark metrics unavailable |
| `cost_report.json` | Monthly DynamoDB cost, pricing notes, reserved capacity, CUR-like items | Mark cost unavailable |

Missing facts → write `Not available in the provided data; verify in the real environment.`

## Detection Rules (apply `rules/overprovisioned_dynamodb.json`)

| Rule | Condition | Severity | Action |
|------|-----------|----------|--------|
| D1 | `PROVISIONED` table/GSI: avg util < 20%, p95 < 50%, no throttling | HIGH | REDUCE_CAPACITY or ADD_AUTOSCALING |
| D2 | `PROVISIONED` low read util under same safety checks | HIGH | REDUCE_RCU or ADD_AUTOSCALING |
| D3 | `PROVISIONED` table/GSI has no Application Auto Scaling target/policy | MEDIUM | ADD_AUTOSCALING |
| D4 | `PAY_PER_REQUEST` with steady high volume; modeled provisioned cost lower | LOW | CONSIDER_PROVISIONED |
| D5 | GSI capacity over-provisioned or missing Auto Scaling | HIGH/MEDIUM | RIGHTSIZE_GSI |
| D6 | Storage, backup, streams, global-table, or table-class cost dominates | INFO | ANALYZE_NON_CAPACITY_COST |

## Safety Guardrails

- Do not reduce capacity or switch billing mode from average utilization alone.
  Check p95/peak, throttling, traffic shape, Auto Scaling, GSIs, RI commitments.
- Sustained throttling → do not reduce; investigate under-provisioning / hot partitions.

Thresholds and billing-mode guidance: `references/details.md` § Utilization Thresholds, § Billing Mode Guidance.

  per evidence; cover both table and GSI. Full rules:
  `references/details.md` § Optimized Terraform.
